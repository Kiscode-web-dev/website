Audience Formation:

To be enlisted to the training courses to obtain primary and extended professional education the candidate must get from the Company an assignment specifying the training program to be attended or he concludes a fee-based contract for training with the Training Centre. In addition to the Contract a copy of a Seafarer’s Identity Document should be attached. Training groups are formed weekly depending on the quantity of applications.

A trainee, taken in a group of primary professional education “Ordinary Seaman – Motorman”, must provide a copy of School-Leaving Certificate, a copy of Passport, Medical Form У-086.

When the course of training is fully paid in due way the candidates are considered as accepted to proceed with further education process. Director of the Training Centre gives an order regarding the date when lessons are to start and their duration, number of participants, assigns teachers and instructors. The training program is included into the Training Centre time-table. Teachers fill in the classes register. The final test usually takes place on completion of the training course (test, exam or test on simulator). The results of test are duly entered into the appropriate exam record/protocol and than, based on the results of these records, the certificates are issued. Students missing the final test, whether for valid reason or not, are allowed to take the final test one more time. On receipt of certificates the trainees acknowledge it putting their signatures in the relevant registration log for issued certificates.
